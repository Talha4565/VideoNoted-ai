Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use VideoNotes AI, we may collect the following minimal data:

  • YouTube URLs you submit for processing (not stored permanently)
  • IP address for rate limiting purposes only (not logged permanently)
  • Usage data via Google Analytics (anonymized page views, session duration)

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information (no login required)
  • We do not store video files or media of any kind
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not use cookies for tracking (only session functionality)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — Transcripts are sent to Google's AI service for processing. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymized usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — Hosting provider. See Railway's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Processed video results are cached in memory for up to 24 hours to improve performance. They are not written to any permanent database and are cleared on server restart.

5. GDPR (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Since we collect minimal data and no account system exists in V1, there is no personal profile to request. For any concerns, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

VideoNotes AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@videonotesai.com
