DMCA Notice & Takedown Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Our Position

VideoNotes AI respects intellectual property rights. We do not host, store, or distribute any video or audio content. Our service only processes publicly available transcript/caption data provided by YouTube's API.

Filing a DMCA Takedown Request

If you believe that content generated by our service infringes your copyright, please send a written notice to:

Email: dmca@videonotesai.com

Your notice must include:

  • Your full legal name and contact information
  • Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • The specific content on our site you claim is infringing
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Response Time

We will review all valid DMCA notices and respond within 5 business days.

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, you may file a counter-notice at the same email address above.

Repeat Infringers

We will terminate access for users who are repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances.
