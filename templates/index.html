          Frequently Asked Questions

          Is this legal?
          Yes. We only access publicly available transcripts/captions that YouTube makes available. We do not download, host, or redistribute any video content.
          Do you download videos?
          No. We never download or store any video files. We only process the text transcript data that YouTube provides publicly.
          What if a transcript is unavailable?
          Some videos don't have captions enabled. In that case, we'll show a clear error message. Try a video that has subtitles or auto-captions enabled.
          Which videos are supported?
          Any public YouTube video that has auto-generated or manually added captions. This covers the vast majority of popular videos.
          Is there a free version?
          Yes! The free tier lets you process 3 videos per day with no signup required. No credit card needed.
          How accurate is the AI summary?
          We use Google Gemini 1.5 Flash, one of the most capable AI models available. Summaries are highly accurate for well-captioned videos.
